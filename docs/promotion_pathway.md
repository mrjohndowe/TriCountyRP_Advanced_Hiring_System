# Promotion Pathway

This document outlines an example internal progression route after a successful hire.

## Entry Stage
- Cadet / Recruit / Trainee
- Shadowing and orientation
- SOP familiarization
- Supervised activity

## Intermediate Stage
- Full member status
- Regular department participation
- Positive radio / scene conduct
- Demonstrates dependable judgment

## Senior Stage
- Trusted member
- Assists with new hires
- Leads scenes appropriately
- Maintains high activity and professionalism

## Supervisory Stage
- Can mentor others
- Helps with training and evaluations
- Resolves low-level conflict
- Understands documentation expectations

## Command Stage
- Strategic department oversight
- Staff development
- Policy input
- Incident review and accountability
