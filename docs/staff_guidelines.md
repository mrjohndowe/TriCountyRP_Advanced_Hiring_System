# Staff Guidelines

All staff involved in recruiting or interviewing must uphold the following standards.

## Conduct Standards

- Show respect to every applicant
- Remain impartial
- Use clear and professional language
- Represent Tri County RP appropriately
- Keep internal decisions confidential until finalized

## Documentation Standards

- Record notes clearly
- Avoid emotional or insulting language
- Focus on observed behavior and answers
- Keep recommendations evidence-based

## Decision-Making Standards

Staff should never:
- Accept someone based on friendship alone
- Deny someone out of personal dislike
- Skip formal evaluation when the system is required
- Misrepresent the final decision
