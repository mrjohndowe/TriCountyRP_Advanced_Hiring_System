# Training Standards

These standards apply after a candidate is accepted.

## Core Expectations

- Trainees must understand department SOPs
- Trainers must document progress accurately
- Training should be fair and repeatable
- Probation should be used for growth, not punishment alone

## Required Training Elements

- Orientation and expectations
- Radio procedure
- Scene conduct
- Documentation process
- Policy review
- Department-specific operational basics

## Trainer Notes

Trainers should assess:
- Attitude
- Coachability
- Confidence
- Decision-making
- Consistency
