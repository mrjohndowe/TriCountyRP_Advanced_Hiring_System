# Probation Recommendations

Use probation when an applicant shows potential but still has concerns that need observation.

## Appropriate Reasons for Probation

- Limited RP experience
- Nervous but coachable communication
- Inconsistent department knowledge
- Average scenario judgment
- Activity concerns that are not severe
- Needs training structure before full trust

## Suggested Probation Terms

### 2 Weeks
Use for minor polish issues.

### 4 Weeks
Use for moderate concerns in judgment, confidence, or knowledge.

### 6 Weeks
Use for applicants needing close observation and documented training progress.

## Probation Requirements

- Attend required trainings
- Maintain respectful conduct
- Meet minimum activity expectations
- Receive positive trainer feedback
- Avoid policy violations

## Probation Outcome Options

- Full acceptance
- Extended probation
- Removal from department
