# Department Standards Overview

## Law Enforcement
Expected strengths:
- Calm scene control
- Proper escalation judgment
- Respectful enforcement RP
- Team coordination

## EMS
Expected strengths:
- Patient-focused communication
- Scene triage awareness
- Department coordination
- Professional bedside conduct

## Fire / Rescue
Expected strengths:
- Resource prioritization
- Safety-first thinking
- Team organization
- Cross-department communication

## Dispatch / Communications
Expected strengths:
- Radio discipline
- Calm under pressure
- Fast prioritization
- Accurate relay of information

## Leadership
Expected strengths:
- Conflict resolution
- Accountability
- Policy awareness
- Team development
- Clear command presence
