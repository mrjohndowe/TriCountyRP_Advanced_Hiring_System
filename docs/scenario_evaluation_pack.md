# Scenario Evaluation Pack

Interviewers should use scenario questions to test judgment, calm thinking, and professionalism.

## Universal Scenarios

### 1. Abuse of Power
You witness a member of your department abusing authority or acting outside policy. What do you do?

**What to look for:**
- Immediate professionalism
- Proper reporting chain
- No escalation through ego
- Scene preservation and documentation

### 2. Escalating Civilian
A civilian becomes increasingly argumentative and attempts to provoke you during an active stop. How do you respond?

**What to look for:**
- De-escalation
- Policy-based action
- Emotional control
- Clear communication

### 3. Multiple Active Calls
You are the highest-ranking unit on at the time and several active calls come in. How do you manage them?

**What to look for:**
- Prioritization
- Delegation
- Radio control
- Scene awareness

## Department Add-Ons

### LEO
A fellow officer rushes force options too early. What do you do in the moment and after the scene?

### EMS
A patient is verbally hostile and refuses help while other casualties remain nearby. How do you prioritize?

### Fire / Rescue
Two major fire-related incidents begin at once with limited apparatus available. How do you assign resources?

### Dispatch
Radio traffic becomes overcrowded and units stop acknowledging properly. How do you regain control?
