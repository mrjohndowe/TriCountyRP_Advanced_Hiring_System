# Leadership Question Bank

## Core Questions

- What would your first priorities be if placed in command?
- How do you handle conflict within your department?
- How do you build accountability without hurting morale?
- How do you address inactivity?
- How do you handle a member who is skilled but repeatedly unprofessional?

## Follow-Ups

- What should command do when policies are unclear?
- How do you encourage retention?
- How do you identify future leaders?

## Scenario

A respected supervisor is creating division in the department, but removing them may hurt morale. How do you approach the issue?
