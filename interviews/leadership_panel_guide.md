# Leadership Interview Panel Guide

Use this guide when interviewing candidates for supervisory, command, or executive roles.

## Leadership Focus Areas

- Department culture
- Accountability
- Policy interpretation
- Conflict management
- Training and retention
- Decision-making under pressure

## Questions

- What does leadership mean to you in an RP community?
- How do you handle underperforming members?
- How would you respond to conflict between two respected members of your department?
- What would your first priorities be if placed in command?
- How do you balance department standards with maintaining morale?
- How do you address favoritism accusations?
- How do you handle a staff member who is technically skilled but disruptive?
- How do you build trust within a department?

## Command Scenario

A department is active, but morale is low and complaints about inconsistency are increasing. What would your first 30 days look like?
