# Interview Quick Reference Sheet

## 60-Second Interview Flow

1. Verify identity and availability
2. Warm up the applicant
3. Confirm RP experience
4. Test professionalism
5. Ask department questions
6. Use at least 2 scenario questions
7. Confirm activity
8. Close professionally
9. Score immediately after interview

## Red Flags

- Rank chasing
- Toxic or dismissive attitude
- No clear understanding of IC vs OOC
- Evasive answers
- Unrealistic activity promises

## Green Flags

- Coachable attitude
- Clear and calm communication
- Real examples from past RP
- Mature conflict handling
- Good teamwork mindset
