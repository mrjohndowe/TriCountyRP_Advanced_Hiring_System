# Full Interview Panel Guide

## Opening Statement

> Welcome to Tri County RP. Today we'll be conducting your interview for the position you applied for. Before we begin, please confirm your name, age, time zone, and Discord username.

## Section 1: Warm-Up

- Tell us a little about yourself.
- What department are you applying for and why?
- What interests you about Tri County RP?
- What would you say you bring to a department team?

## Section 2: Experience

- What previous RP experience do you have?
- Have you held any ranks or leadership positions before?
- What type of RP do you enjoy most?
- What is an example of a strong RP scene you were part of?
- What did that experience teach you?

## Section 3: Professionalism

- How do you handle low-effort or troll RP?
- How do you separate IC conflict from OOC feelings?
- What does professionalism mean to you in a department?
- How do you handle feedback or criticism from higher-ups?
- How would you respond to a disagreement with another member?

## Section 4: Department-Specific

Use the applicable file from `department_questions/`.

## Section 5: Scenario Evaluation

- You witness an officer or department member abusing power. What do you do?
- A civilian escalates during a stop or scene. How do you de-escalate?
- You're the highest-ranking member on duty and multiple calls come in at once. How do you manage the situation?
- Another member begins breaking server rules mid-scene. What is your response?

## Section 6: Activity & Commitment

- How active can you realistically be each week?
- Are you comfortable attending meetings or trainings?
- What times are you usually available?
- Do you foresee any limits that may affect your consistency?

## Closing

> Do you have any questions for us before we wrap up?

> Thank you for your time. We'll review your answers and get back to you shortly.
